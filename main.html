Bianca Matthews is a sophomore at University of Miami. She has been working as an actress since 8 yrs old starring in films, television, regional theater and innumerable commercials . A natural leader, she has had management positions in business since the age of 15. Her keen interest and love of video games and technology led her to majoring in computer science, interactive media and game design.
